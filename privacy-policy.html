Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected
Personal Data

In order to provide our services, we may ask for the following personally identifiable information:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Phone number
  • Usage Data

Usage Data: Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service and may include details like your device’s IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, device identifiers, and other diagnostic data. When using a mobile device, we may collect additional information such as your mobile device’s unique ID, operating system, mobile browser type, and IP address.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our Service and store certain information. These technologies include:

  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device. You can set your browser to reject all cookies, but this may limit your ability to use parts of our Service.
  • Web Beacons: Small electronic files used to count users who have visited certain pages or opened emails, and track other related site statistics.

Cookies can be classified as:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and are deleted once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device after you close your browser, helping us remember your preferences.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: To provide essential features, such as authentication and preventing fraudulent activities.
  • Cookies Policy Acceptance: To track if you have accepted our cookie policy.
  • Functionality Cookies: To remember your preferences and enhance user experience.

For more details on cookies, please refer to our [Cookie Policy].
